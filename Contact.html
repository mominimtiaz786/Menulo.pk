Rate & comment
By using the Evaluate function, it is possible to evaluate the respective product or products that should contribute to product improvement. This function is voluntary and requires the first name, last name, the comment and the rating in the form of stars from 1 to 5 while 5 is very good or very satisfied for rating & commenting on the products. When rating, the first name, last name as well as the rating in the form of stars are saved on the page and can be seen publicly, while the written comment with first name, last name and rating by email to the owner and operator of the respective restaurant is sent. This is sent to the respective restaurateur for the purpose of the product improvement and is also intended exclusively for this.

For reasons of misuse, the comment itself is not published on the website, for this reason the IP address of every visitor of the reviews is saved in order to prevent misuse of this functionality. The function should help the restaurateurs and visitors and should not be used for abuse. The use of this evaluation function is voluntary. You therefore consent to the transmission of this data every time you evaluate it. The caterer who receives this data is obliged to handle it with care and also responsible for not disclosing the data to third parties, but only to treat it for the use of the product improvement, as this should contribute to the product improvement. He is responsible for this data, which the respective restaurateur receives by email, and not Menulo.
